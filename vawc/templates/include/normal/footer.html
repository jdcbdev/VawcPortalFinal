About VAWC

The VAWC Desk Portal is a web application that helps authorities track and monitor VAWC cases in the region. This initiative, in partnership with Western Mindanao State University (WMSU) and the Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG) Region IX, is dedicated to providing protection, advocacy, and resources to combat violence and promote equality.
